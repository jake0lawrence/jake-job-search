# Enablement & Adoption

## Role Definition

Roles focused on driving customer adoption through education, training, and enablement programs. Emphasis on curriculum development, content creation, program design, and measuring adoption outcomes rather than account management.

### Typical Titles

- Customer Enablement Manager
- Adoption Program Manager
- Customer Education Manager
- Customer Training Manager
- Product Adoption Manager
- Customer Programs Manager

### What They're Looking For

- Training program design and delivery
- Curriculum development experience
- Content creation (written, video, interactive)
- Adoption measurement and optimization
- Scalable program thinking
- Train-the-trainer methodology

## Interview Preparation

### Key Themes to Hit

1. **Scale:** "120+ training sessions to 650+ users with 4.8/5 satisfaction"
2. **Design thinking:** "I design programs, not just deliver training"
3. **Measurement:** "I track adoption outcomes, not just completion"
4. **Methodology:** "Train-the-trainer expands reach beyond my direct delivery"

### Likely Questions

| Question                                       | Your Angle                                                      |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "How do you design a training program?"        | Audience analysis, role-based tracks, delivery mix, measurement |
| "How do you measure training effectiveness?"   | Adoption metrics, not just satisfaction; behavior change        |
| "Tell me about a time you scaled enablement"   | Office hours, playbooks, 1:many programs                        |
| "How do you handle different learning styles?" | Multiple formats, self-serve + live, role-based depth           |

### Questions to Ask Them

- "How is customer enablement structured here?"
- "What does success look like for this role?"
- "What tools/platforms do you use for content delivery?"
- "How do you measure adoption outcomes?"
