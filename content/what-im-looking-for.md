# What I'm Looking For

## The Roles (In Plain English)

### 1. Customer Success Manager (CSM)
**What it is:** After a company buys software, the CSM makes sure they actually succeed with it. You're their main point of contact, you check in regularly, you help them solve problems, and you make sure they renew their contract.

**Why I'm good at it:** I've been doing this for 5+ years. I've managed portfolios of 100+ customers, built health dashboards to spot problems early, and resolved executive escalations without losing customers.

**Salary range:** $90-140K

**Look for titles like:**
- Customer Success Manager
- Client Success Manager
- Customer Experience Manager
- Account Manager (when it's not sales-focused)

---

### 2. Implementation Consultant
**What it is:** When a company buys new software, someone needs to set it up. That's this role. You work with the customer to understand their needs, configure the software, migrate their data, train their team, and get them live.

**Why I'm good at it:** I've done 100+ implementations. 92% on-time, 95% on-budget. This is my wheelhouse.

**Salary range:** $90-130K

**Look for titles like:**
- Implementation Consultant
- Implementation Specialist
- Solutions Consultant
- Professional Services Consultant
- Onboarding Specialist

---

### 3. Enablement / Training Manager
**What it is:** Designing and delivering training programs that help customers use software effectively. This could be webinars, documentation, certification programs, or in-person training.

**Why I'm good at it:** I've delivered 120+ training sessions to 650+ people with a 4.8/5 satisfaction rating. I build programs that scale.

**Salary range:** $95-150K

**Look for titles like:**
- Customer Enablement Manager
- Customer Education Manager
- Training Manager
- Adoption Program Manager
- Customer Programs Manager

## The Company Profile

**Ideal company:**
- **Size:** 100-5,000 employees (big enough to have structure, small enough to matter)
- **Stage:** Growth stage or established (not super early startup)
- **Product:** Software that requires implementation and ongoing support (not self-serve)
- **Culture:** Values customer outcomes, not just sales numbers

**I'm flexible on:**
- Industry (tech, fintech, healthcare, etc.)
- Remote vs. hybrid vs. office
- Exact title

**I'm firm on:**
- Must be a product company (not an agency/consultancy)
- Must have a real CS or Implementation function
- Must be base-salary-heavy (not commission-dependent)
